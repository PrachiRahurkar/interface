1. BERT is a state-of-the-art language model released by Google in 2018. It can be used for a variety of natural language tasks. It is trained on billion words from the Wikipedia corpora.

2. Here, we use BERT for the task of Question-Answering. Given a paragraph and a question, BERT answers the question based on the para.

3. Your job is to provide a perturbed paragraph to the trained model. And then hit predict.

4. Modify the paragraph, such that the correct answer is still in the paragraph.

(Meaning, edit the para in such a way that a human can still correctly answer the ground truth from your para. But the model cannot.)

5. You can find some examples of adversaries which are successfully able to fool the model in the tab "Adversarial attack examples".


Edit the Custom Paragraph in the textbox. After you are done editing, click Predict.

Submit your example when you succeed in fooling the model.

( Remember to add/delete to the paragraph without changing the Ground Truth. )

Enjoy!
